# Mobile Robots

## Definition

This hardware class covers general wheeled or ground mobile robots that are not primarily racecars, quadrupeds, or manipulators.

## Relationship to the Wiki

This page belongs naturally near [[cognitive_navigation]], [[embodied_autonomous_intelligence]], and [[real_world_robotic_reinforcement_learning]].

In this vault, mobile robots often appear in exploration, navigation, and planning papers where the emphasis is less on pure top speed than on embodied autonomy in larger or less structured environments.
