# Table Tennis Robots

## Definition

This hardware class covers robotic systems built to perceive, reach, strike, and rally in table-tennis settings.

## Relationship to the Wiki

This page belongs naturally near [[robot_table_tennis]], [[high_speed_perception]], and [[reinforcement_learning]].

In this vault, table-tennis robots matter as a compact but extremely demanding test of real-time embodied intelligence: the timing is fast, the sensing is noisy, the opponent is adaptive, and the cost of late action is immediate failure.
