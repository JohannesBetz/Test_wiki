# Quadrupeds

## Definition

This hardware class covers four-legged robots such as Mini Cheetah, ANYmal-like systems, and other legged platforms used to demonstrate agile locomotion, navigation, or interaction.

## Relationship to the Wiki

This page belongs naturally near [[quadrupedal_locomotion]], [[Highly_dynamic_autonomous_system]], and [[embodied_autonomous_intelligence]].

Quadrupeds matter in this vault because they show that extreme-environment autonomy is not only a wheeled or aerial problem. They expose the same deeper challenge of maintaining useful speed and stability while the embodiment, terrain, and contact dynamics remain tightly coupled.
