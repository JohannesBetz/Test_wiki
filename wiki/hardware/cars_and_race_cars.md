# Cars and Race Cars

## Definition

This hardware class covers full-scale racecars, passenger-car-like autonomous test vehicles, scaled racecars such as F1TENTH platforms, and simulator-centered racecar embodiments when the paper's demonstrated behavior is clearly anchored to car dynamics.

## Relationship to the Wiki

This page belongs naturally near [[autonomous_vehicle_racing]], [[autonomous_racing_platforms]], and [[model_predictive_control]].

In this vault, cars and race cars are the dominant hardware family because they expose the classic high-speed-autonomy problems of grip limits, state estimation, planning under interaction, and near-limit control.
