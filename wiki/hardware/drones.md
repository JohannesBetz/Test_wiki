# Drones

## Definition

This hardware class covers quadrotors, FPV racing drones, MAVs, and other aerial robotic platforms used to demonstrate fast embodied autonomy.

## Relationship to the Wiki

This page belongs naturally near [[autonomous_drone_racing]], [[high_speed_perception]], and [[real_world_robotic_reinforcement_learning]].

In this vault, drones are one of the clearest testbeds for highly dynamic autonomy because their short time constants, narrow margins, and sensing constraints make weak control or weak perception fail quickly.
