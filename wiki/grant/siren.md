## Objective

SIREN aims to develop a holistic view of robot learning built around structured interactive perception and embodied intelligence.

According to the CORDIS fact sheet, the project asks how robots can learn by integrating themselves and their surroundings as one cohesive system rather than treating perception, learning, and embodiment as loosely connected modules.

The keywords exposed in the grant material are especially revealing:

- robot learning,
- multisensory perception,
- graph neural networks,
- and mobile manipulation.

So the project appears to sit at the intersection of perception-rich robot learning and broader embodied interaction, rather than inside one narrow locomotion or manipulation niche.
