# TORCS

## Definition

TORCS, The Open Racing Car Simulator, is a lightweight 3D racing simulator with tracks, vehicles, opponents, and vehicle physics.

## Key Approaches

[[autonomous_vehicles_on_the_edge]] identifies TORCS as a recurring environment for autonomous racing research in control, trajectory planning, game theory, and [[reinforcement_learning]].
