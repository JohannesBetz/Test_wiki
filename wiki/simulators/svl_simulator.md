# SVL Simulator

## Definition

SVL Simulator is a 3D autonomous vehicle simulation platform with maps, vehicles, sensor modeling, weather simulation, and APIs for autonomy stacks such as Autoware.Auto and Baidu Apollo.

## Key Approaches

[[autonomous_vehicles_on_the_edge]] notes that SVL includes vehicle models for [[f1tenth]] and [[indy_autonomous_challenge]]-style racing, making it relevant for autonomous racing simulation and reinforcement learning.
