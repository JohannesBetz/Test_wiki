# SONIC

## What It Is

`SONIC` is a humanoid motion-tracking framework aimed at natural whole-body control.

## Why It Matters

This system matters because it pushes the humanoid branch toward a larger and more natural motion-tracking substrate rather than only isolated skill demos.

For this vault, SONIC is useful as a model/system anchor for:

- large-scale humanoid motion tracking,
- natural whole-body humanoid control,
- sim-to-real humanoid deployment,
- and the expansion of motion tracking into a broader control foundation.
