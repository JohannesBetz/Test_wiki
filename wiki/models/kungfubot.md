# KungfuBot

## What It Is

`KungfuBot` is a physics-based humanoid whole-body control framework for learning and executing highly dynamic human-like skills such as Kungfu and dancing.

## Why It Matters

This system matters because it broadens the humanoid branch from locomotion and parkour into expressive whole-body skill imitation under dynamic physical constraints.

For this vault, KungfuBot is useful as a model/system anchor for:

- highly dynamic humanoid skill imitation,
- adaptive motion tracking,
- reference-motion refinement,
- and deployment of expressive whole-body behaviors on a real humanoid.
