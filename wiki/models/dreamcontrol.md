# DreamControl

## What It Is

`DreamControl` is a humanoid whole-body control system for scene interaction built around guided diffusion.

## Why It Matters

This system matters because it adds a generative-model route to the humanoid branch, especially for natural whole-body interaction with scenes.

For this vault, DreamControl is useful as a model/system anchor for:

- human-inspired whole-body humanoid control,
- scene interaction,
- guided diffusion for embodied behavior,
- and generative priors for whole-body action.
