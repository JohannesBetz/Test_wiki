# PhysHSI

## What It Is

`PhysHSI` is a humanoid-scene interaction system aimed at real-world generalizable and natural whole-body behavior.

## Why It Matters

This system matters because it pushes the humanoid branch beyond locomotion, balance, and parkour into grounded interaction with real scenes.

For this vault, PhysHSI is useful as a model/system anchor for:

- real-world humanoid-scene interaction,
- AMP-style motor-prior learning,
- embodied whole-body behavior under scene grounding,
- and the transition from isolated skill demos toward broader humanoid deployment stacks.
