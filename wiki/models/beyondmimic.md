# BeyondMimic

## What It Is

`BeyondMimic` is a guided-diffusion humanoid control approach that aims to move from motion tracking toward more versatile whole-body control.

## Why It Matters

This system matters because it gives the humanoid branch a concrete answer to a recurring question in the vault: how do we turn strong imitation or tracking into broader capability rather than stopping there?

For this vault, BeyondMimic is useful as a model/system anchor for:

- guided-diffusion humanoid control,
- the transition from motion tracking to control versatility,
- whole-body humanoid behavior beyond narrow mimicry,
- and richer generative priors for embodied action.
