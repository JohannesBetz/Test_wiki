# Roborace

## Definition

Roborace is a full-size autonomous racing platform and competition effort built around standardized autonomous racecars such as Devbot and Robocar.

## Key Approaches

[[autonomous_vehicles_on_the_edge]] describes Roborace as a major source of work on localization, mapping, planning, control, energy management, simulation, scenario generation, vehicle modeling, and complete racing software stacks.
