# Barkour

## What It Is

`Barkour` is a quadruped agility benchmark built as a compact obstacle course with a time-and-penalty scoring system inspired by dog agility competitions.

## Why It Matters

This benchmark gives the wiki a shared reference point for animal-level quadruped agility.

Instead of evaluating agility only through isolated speed demos, Barkour emphasizes:

- obstacle diversity,
- controllability,
- versatility,
- and completion efficiency under one score.

That makes it a useful complement to the vault's quadruped papers, many of which demonstrate strong behaviors but do not all share the same evaluation frame.
