# Formula Student Driverless

## Definition

Formula Student Driverless is a student engineering competition for autonomous race vehicles. Teams design the vehicle, sensor suite, compute stack, and autonomy software.

## Key Approaches

[[autonomous_vehicles_on_the_edge]] links Formula Student Driverless to cone detection, SLAM/localization, planning, control, and complete software-stack papers. The competition includes acceleration, skidpad, autocross, trackdrive, efficiency, business, cost, and design events.
