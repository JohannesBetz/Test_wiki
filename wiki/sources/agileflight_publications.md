# AGILEFLIGHT Publications Source

> Source: `raw/grants/ERC_ConsodilatorGrant_Scaramuzza__AgileFlight_AllPapers.pdf`
> Grant Acronym: [[agileflight|AGILEFLIGHT]]
> Research Group: Robotics and Perception Group, University of Zurich (Davide Scaramuzza)

## Summary

This source is a compiled record of 72 publications (spanning 2015 to 2026) produced under or associated with the ERC Consolidator Grant [[agileflight]]. It acts as a comprehensive index of the Scaramuzza Lab's breakthroughs in autonomous drone flight, low-latency vision, event-based tracking, hybrid modeling, and the integration of reinforcement learning with model predictive control.

## Main Research Areas

The grant's output covers five central pillars:
1. **Event-Based Vision & Control**: Harnessing event cameras for microsecond latency and zero motion blur in aggressive environments.
2. **RL vs. MPC Control**: Exploring how to combine model-free RL task performance with MPC constraint satisfaction (e.g., [[actor_critic_model_predictive_control_differentiable_optimization_meets_reinforcement_learning_for_agile_flight]]).
3. **Differentiable Simulation**: Boosting RL sample efficiency via gradient backpropagation (for example, *Learning Quadrotor Control From Visual Features Using Differentiable Simulation*).
4. **Preference-Based Learning & Acrobatics**: Learning dynamic maneuvers without manual reward shaping.
5. **Real-World Adaptation & Platforms**: Designing open hardware platforms (e.g., [[agilicious_open_source_and_open_hardware_agile_quadrotor_for_vision_based_flight]]) and online residual adaptation.
